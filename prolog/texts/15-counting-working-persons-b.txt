There are exactly three students and there are exactly two lecturers.

The first student is John.

The second student is Bob.

The first lecturer is Alice.

Every student and every lecturer is a person.

John works and Bob does not work.

The third student works.

Alice works.

If a person does not provably work then the person does not work.

How many persons work?

How many persons do not work?

Who works?

Who does not work?
